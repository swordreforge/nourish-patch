Nourish

A simple next-gen desktop for work.

See it in action

Keep everything in order

Gather a handful of windows into a named group — line them up, space them evenly, fullscreen one within the group, or collapse the whole bunch down to its name. And nothing gets lost: when an app closes or crashes, Nourish leaves a placeholder right where it was, and one tap brings it back — same spot, same size. Your whole layout is saved to disk, so it survives a reboot too.

The world picker

Keep separate canvases for separate jobs. A keyboard shortcut raises a gentle 3D picker with every world shown as a tile — fly into any one, and each keeps its own windows and remembers itself between sessions.

Capture anything

Screenshot or record a window, a region of the canvas that follows your pan and zoom, or the whole screen. Save a crisp PNG or a hardware-encoded H.264 video — even with a transparent background for clean overlays.
